About Me


Born in the tail end of the 60s, I have been fortunate enough to live in interesting times. After 10 years as a Naval Nuclear Engineering Supervisor, I was convinced by a good friend to come visit him in Tampa for the weekend. After eight years of bartending that weekend, I returned home to Las Vegas, where I managed for Barnes & Noble. I would return to bartending, opening several bars and working as district trainer for a few corporate Steak Houses. My latest open, The Git Out, maintains a 4.9 rating on Yelp! and has the premiere bourbon selection south of the river here in Austin. I'm not an advid gamer per se, but I enjoy modding games and watching streamers complain about their favorite games. Coding is a wonderful series of puzzles to be solved with elegance, and as the saying goes, it's not work if you love what you're doing!
